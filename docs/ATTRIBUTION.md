**Yes.** Provided you:
1. Include the complete LICENSE.md
2. Preserve all copyright notices
3. Follow the license terms
4. Give proper attribution

### Can I modify COBOL Protocol?
**Yes.** Provided you:
1. Preserve copyright headers

### Can I distribute COBOL Protocol?
